Page Definitions

Login

Allows existing users to authenticate and access their account using credentials.

Registration

Enables new users to create an account by providing required personal and account information.

Products

Displays available furniture items with descriptions, images, and options to add items to cart.

Catalog

Provides a comprehensive listing of all furniture products organized by category.

Order

Manages and displays user's placed orders with details and status information.

Payment

Handles payment processing and collection of billing information for purchases.
