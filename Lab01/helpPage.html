Help & Page Definitions

Pages Overview

Login

Allows existing users to authenticate and access their account using their registered credentials.

Registration

Enables new users to create an account by providing required personal and account information.

Products

Displays available furniture items with descriptions and options to place an order directly.

Catalog

Provides a comprehensive listing of all furniture products and allows admins to add new items.

Order

Manages and captures delivery details for a selected product to complete a purchase.

Payment

Handles payment processing and collection of billing information for customer purchases.
